About Us

Our journey began with a shared passion for technology and a vision to provide enthusiasts and professionals alike with the perfect computing solution. Maxiware emerged as a personal project founded by a group of friends and people with the same interests in tech and the PC building culture. From our humble beginnings as just an idea among friends in university, we've grown into a trusted name through our customers. Our story is one of continuous growth, fueled by the unwavering belief that everyone deserves a computer that meets their unique needs and aspirations.

Our Team

Our Team


At the core of Maxiware is a team of skilled technicians, dedicated PC builders, and passionate tech experts. We are united by our love for cutting-edge hardware and our commitment to providing you with the best PC-building experience possible.

Our team brings an abundance of innovative ideas and quality executions, ensuring that every PC we build is a reflection of our dedication to quality, performance, and customer satisfaction. We work tirelessly to stay at the forefront of technology so that we can provide you with the latest advancements in the world of computing.

Our Delivery

Our Delivery


We understand that timely delivery is crucial, and we take this commitment seriously. When you order a PC from Maxiware, you can expect:

Prompt Processing: Your order is processed efficiently, and our team works diligently to assemble and test your PC within the promised timeframe.

Careful Packaging: Your PC is packed securely to ensure it arrives in pristine condition, ready for you to unbox and enjoy.

Our Services

Our Services


Maxiware offers a comprehensive range of services designed to cater to a diverse audience of gamers, content creators, professionals, and everyday users. Our services include:

Custom PC Building: Tailor your PC to your exact specifications with our easy-to-use configurator and expert guidance.

Pre-Built PCs: Choose from our carefully curated selection of pre-built PCs, ready to deliver exceptional performance out of the box.

Customer Support: Our team of experts is always ready to assist you, providing guidance, support, and technical advice throughout your PC-building journey.
