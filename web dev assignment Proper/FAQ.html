Frequently Asked Questions

What is a custom-built PC, and why should I choose Maxiware?
A custom-built PC is a computer system assembled with components chosen by the user to meet their specific needs. You should use Maxiware if you want a computer with the highest quality components, a smooth and simple way to create your own custom PC tailored to your exact requirements. Choosing Maxiware is the best choice when getting started in PC building because of our easy and user friendly interface.
How do I start building my custom PC with Maxiware?
Starting the process is easy! You can go to Build and select your preferred components to start building your dream PC that matches all your specifications and budget. If you need assistance, our team of experts is available to guide you through the process through our live chat feature.
What components can I customize in my PC?
You can customize various components, including the CPU, GPU, RAM, storage, motherboard, cooling system, and more. Our selection allows you to mix and match components to create your ideal PC.
Can I get a pre-built PC from Maxiware?
Absolutely! We offer a range of pre-built PCs for different use cases, from gaming to content creation. These systems are expertly assembled and tested, ready to use right out of the box!
How long does it take to build and deliver a custom PC?
The build time varies depending on the complexity of your custom PC and component availability. Typically, it takes anywhere from 7 to 14 business days.
Do you offer warranty and support for your PCs?
Yes, we provide warranties for all our custom-built and pre-built PCs. The length and terms of the warranty depend on the components and services chosen. Additionally, we offer comprehensive customer support to address any post-purchase concerns or issues.
How does Maxiware operate?
Maxiware functions by sourcing our components from legitimate suppliers and gathering and shipping out the components that the users pick out.
